About

Psychology, data, and clear communication.

I bring a mix of psychology, statistics, and hands on analytics experience into every project.

Chibuike Okerulu (BK) · Data analytics and BI consultant based in Douglasville, GA.

My path into data started in psychology. Through research methods and statistics, I learned how to design studies, work with messy human data, and explain complex patterns to non technical people.

Today I work as a Data Analyst and BI Consultant. I build clean data models, SQL pipelines, and dashboards that help teams see what is happening in their business and what to do next.

I enjoy collaborating with product managers, clinicians, and executives, clarifying the real question behind a request, then turning it into something measurable and trackable.

How I work

  • Start with the business question and define success clearly.
  • Design data models and dashboards around that question.
  • Translate insights into simple, concrete next steps.

What I enjoy

  • Pairing SQL and Python with tools like Power BI and Tableau.
