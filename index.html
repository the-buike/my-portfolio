About

Psychology, data, and clear communication.

I bring a mix of psychology, statistics, and hands on analytics experience into every project.

Chibuike Okerulu (BK) · Data analytics and BI consultant based in Douglasville, GA.

My path into data started in psychology. Through research methods and statistics I learned how to design studies, work with messy human data, and explain complex patterns to non-technical people.

Today I work as a Data Analyst and BI Consultant: I build reliable data models, SQL pipelines, and dashboards that help teams see what is happening and decide what to do next.

Recently I enrolled in the TripleTen AI & Machine Learning bootcamp to deepen my practical ML skills — from feature engineering and model training to evaluation and deployment. The program reinforced how to apply ML responsibly and tie model results back to real business decisions through experiments and clear documentation.

I enjoy collaborating with product managers, clinicians, and executives to clarify the real question, scope measurable goals, and deliver solutions that stakeholders can trust and act on.

How I work

  • Start with the business question and define measurable success criteria.
  • Prototype quickly (data samples, sketches) to validate assumptions with stakeholders.
  • Build reproducible data models and pipelines for reliability and maintainability.
  • Iterate with users — deliver early, gather feedback, and refine visualizations and metrics.
  • Document decisions and tests so outcomes are transparent and repeatable.

What I enjoy

  • Structuring messy datasets into reliable, reusable models and pipelines.
  • Combining SQL and Python with BI tools (Power BI, Tableau) to tell clear stories.
  • Building dashboards that change how teams make decisions.
  • Experimenting with small ML prototypes and model interpretability techniques.
  • Teaching stakeholders how to read, question, and trust the data.

Domains
